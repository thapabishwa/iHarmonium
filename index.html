How to Play

  • Allow camera access — the webcam detects lid angle via brightness
  • Slowly close and open your lid during the 3-second calibration
  • Open lid = bright = full air — close lid = dark = no air
  • Or hold Space to pump manually if camera is denied
  • Play notes: A-; (white keys), W-P (black keys)
  • Shift octave: Z (down) / X (up)
